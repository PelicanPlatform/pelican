## pelican job status

Get the status of a transfer job

### Synopsis

Get detailed status information about a transfer job, including
the status of all transfers within the job and overall progress.

```
pelican job status <job-id> [flags]
```

### Options

```
  -h, --help    help for status
  -w, --watch   Watch job status until completion
```
