End User AUP

The operators of the OSG Fabric of Services have legal obligations to protect shared services and resources. You share this responsibility by observing and upholding the rules of acceptable use that are outlined in this document.

*The "OSG Fabric of Services" includes, but is not limited to*

- *SSH or web access to Open Science Pool (OSPool) Access Points*
- *Ability to place jobs to run on the OSPool*
- *Ability to stage and access data through the Open Science Data Federation (OSDF)*
- *SSH access and the ability to place jobs to run on the PATh Facility.*

Your online assent to this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is your acknowledgment that you have read, understand and accept your responsibilities when using OSG Fabric of Services. If you have questions, please contact support@osg-htc.org.

Violations of this AUP may result in loss of access to services. Activities in violation of any laws will be reported to the proper authorities for investigation and prosecution.

By using OSG Fabric of Services, you agree to comply with the following conditions of use:

1. The services may only be used for the purpose of research or education/training. As a condition for access, you may be asked to provide a general description of the planned activities (e.g., lead investigator, field of science, project goals) that will be made publicly available on an OSG website.

1. OSG Fabric of Services does not provide services or resources that satisfy the security requirements for handling or processing HIPAA, PII, CUI, FERPA, or any other data protected by statute or data-use agreement. You agree to not introduce these types of data to OSG Fabric of Services. You have the responsibility for compliance with all data use agreements and other restrictions. By using the services you assert that you are in compliance with all laws, including copyright, and terms of services for data you place on the service.

1. You understand that OSG Fabric of Services does not guarantee that resources and services will be available, nor that they will suit every purpose, nor that data will never be lost or corrupted. These services are run best-effort, typically as part of research projects. Use of OSG Fabric of Services is at your own risk.

1. The OSG Fabric of Services is not responsible for data persistence. Best effort is done to avoid data loss but the services may incur data loss due to operator error, cybersecurity events, or to preserve operational integrity. You are responsible for backing up your data. In the event of ransomware attacks, all data will be lost. Data access protection is best effort by service operators; service operators have no liability if data is exposed to others whether due to negligence, software bugs / misconfigurations, or malicious activity. Service operators make no claim to ownership of any data or outputs produced using the OSG Fabric of Services.

1. You are responsible for securely managing your login credentials (including, but not limited to SSH keys, university login in credentials, or credentials from another SSO provider). This includes not sharing your login credentials with any other person, including anyone representing OSG services. The private key of any generated keypair should always be encrypted.

1. You agree not to use OSG Fabric of Services for any of the following:

- financial gain

- any unlawful purpose

- cryptomining

- attempt to breach or circumvent any service's administrative or security controls, within the OSG Fabric of Services or external services.

- violation of any agreement or terms-and-conditions of software or data introduced to an OSG service

  You agree to comply with all applicable laws and relevant regulations, such as export control laws.

7. You agree to immediately report any known or suspected security breach or misuse of your access to security@osg-htc.org.

1. Logged information, including information provided by you for registration purposes, is used internally for administrative, operational, accounting, monitoring and security purposes. Following the UW - Madison Privacy Notice (https://www.wisc.edu/privacy-notice/), this data is only used for the purposes for which it was collected unless the service operator reasonably considers that it is needed for another related reason and that the reason is compatible with the original purpose. You authorize the collection and sharing of this data for the purposes described above.

1. There is no guarantee, implicit or explicit, of access to the service. As part of a security or AUP violation investigation or to preserve operational stability, service operators may suspend your access at any time.

When you receive access to OSG Fabric of Services, you will be asked to accept and re-acknowledge your responsibilities regarding use of OSG Fabric of Services once a year.

You shall provide appropriate acknowledgement of support or citation for your use of the resources/services provided as required by the body or bodies granting you access. More information on citing the OSPool can be found here: [https://osg-htc.org/acknowledging](https://osg-htc.org/acknowledging).
