# Acceptable Use Policy (default v1.0)

The operators of this Pelican federation have legal obligations to protect shared services and resources. You share this responsibility by observing and upholding the rules of acceptable use that are outlined in this document.

*Pelican Federation Services include, but are not limited to:*

- *Web access to the federation's services and dashboards*
- *Ability to place data into and access data through the federation's data plane*
- *Use of any compute, storage, or networking resources advertised through this federation*

Your online assent to this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is your acknowledgement that you have read, understand and accept your responsibilities when using these services.

Violations of this AUP may result in loss of access to services. Activities in violation of any laws will be reported to the proper authorities for investigation and prosecution.

By using these services, you agree to comply with the following conditions of use:

1. The services may only be used for the purpose of research or education/training. As a condition for access, you may be asked to provide a general description of the planned activities (e.g., lead investigator, field of science, project goals) that may be made publicly available on the federation's website.

1. The federation does not provide services or resources that satisfy the security requirements for handling or processing HIPAA, PII, CUI, FERPA, or any other data protected by statute or data-use agreement. You agree to not introduce these types of data into the federation. You have the responsibility for compliance with all data-use agreements and other restrictions. By using the services you assert that you are in compliance with all laws, including copyright, and terms of services for data you place on the service.

1. You understand that the federation does not guarantee that resources and services will be available, nor that they will suit every purpose, nor that data will never be lost or corrupted. These services are run best-effort, typically as part of research projects. Use of the federation is at your own risk.

1. The federation is not responsible for data persistence. Best effort is done to avoid data loss but the services may incur data loss due to operator error, cybersecurity events, or to preserve operational integrity. You are responsible for backing up your data. Data-access protection is best effort by service operators; service operators have no liability if data is exposed to others whether due to negligence, software bugs / misconfigurations, or malicious activity. Service operators make no claim to ownership of any data or outputs produced using these services.

1. You are responsible for securely managing your login credentials (including, but not limited to university login credentials, or credentials from another SSO provider). This includes not sharing your login credentials with any other person, including anyone representing the federation's services. The private key of any generated keypair should always be encrypted.

1. You agree not to use these services for any of the following:

   - financial gain
   - any unlawful purpose
   - cryptomining
   - attempts to breach or circumvent any service's administrative or security controls, within the federation or in external services
   - violation of any agreement or terms-and-conditions of software or data introduced to the federation

   You agree to comply with all applicable laws and relevant regulations, such as export-control laws.

1. You agree to immediately report any known or suspected security breach or misuse of your access to the federation's security contact.

1. Logged information, including information provided by you for registration purposes, is used internally for administrative, operational, accounting, research, monitoring, and security purposes. This data is only used for the purposes for which it was collected unless the service operator reasonably considers that it is needed for another related reason and that the reason is compatible with the original purpose. You authorize the collection and sharing of this data for the purposes described above.

1. There is no guarantee, implicit or explicit, of access to the service. As part of a security or AUP violation investigation or to preserve operational stability, service operators may suspend your access at any time.

When you receive access to the federation, you may be asked to accept and re-acknowledge your responsibilities regarding use of the federation once a year.

You shall provide appropriate acknowledgement of support or citation for your use of the resources/services provided as required by the body or bodies granting you access.
